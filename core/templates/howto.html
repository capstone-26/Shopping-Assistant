How does this all work? Its simple, let me show you!

To begin, you will need to create a watchlist by going to the watchlist tab.

Click "Create new watchlist" to add a watchlist to your account


------------------------------------------------------------------

Give the watchlist a name and click "Create", or just hit enter!


------------------------------------------------------------------

Congrats! You now have a watchlist to use and track products across Coles, Woolworths and Aldi!

Click into the watchlist to start adding products


Alternatively, click "Delete" to remove the watchlist if it is no longer needed

------------------------------------------------------------------

Click into the "Add Product to watchlist" to start adding products


------------------------------------------------------------------

Enter the product you are looking for and hit enter


========


Alternatively, search for the product in the top right of any page on the website

------------------------------------------------------------------

Look at all these Options!

Select the product you would like to add to your watchlist by clicking the "Add" button next to the product


------------------------------------------------------------------

WooHoo, the product has been successfully added to the watchlist!


You can also see the total current price of the watchlist at the bottom of the table to help you keep track

------------------------------------------------------------------

Select the "Remove" button to remove the product from the watchlist if it is no longer needed

------------------------------------------------------------------

"But I want more information about the product and want to compare my choice with other options?"

No Issue. Search for a product the same way we did before and instead of clicking "Add", click on the product name


------------------------------------------------------------------

You can now see product details and suggestions of similar products you can compare your product with

Click the "Compare" button next to the product you would like to compare to bring up a comparison of the 2 products

------------------------------------------------------------------

Save the latest price by clicking the "Save Price" button next to the product price


------------------------------------------------------------------

You can now see the price you just saved, along with all the old prices next to their dates in the table below


------------------------------------------------------------------


It's as easy as that! To get started and create your own watchlist by going to the watchlist tab.
